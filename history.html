Our History


Life-Elixir Care Foundation was incorporated in July 2023 with a vision to make a meaningful impact on the lives of those in need. Witnessing numerous children deprived of basic education due to financial constraints and the struggles of enthusiastic youths with viable business ideas but lacking financial resources, we were inspired to take action. Our mission extended beyond the young, recognizing the importance of addressing the needs of the elderly who often receive less attention in our society.


At its core, our foundation aims to empower the less privileged through a multifaceted approach. We believe that education is a powerful catalyst for change, and to achieve this, we focus on providing scholarships and educational opportunities for underprivileged children. By investing in their education, we strive to empower them to become future leaders and change agents in their communities.


Furthermore, we understand the significance of keeping young people engaged and away from negative influences. Through skill acquisition training, enlightenment programs, and awareness initiatives, we guide them towards success and away from social vices. Our commitment to the younger generation is unwavering, as we believe in nurturing their potential and providing a path to a brighter future.


Additionally, our organization is driven by a sense of responsibility towards the elderly. Recognizing the poor care given to older individuals, leading to high mortality rates, we established our caring for the elderly division. Through this initiative, we collaborate with healthcare institutions to ensure that the elderly receive quality medical care and are provided with state-of-the-art homes where they can receive the attention and care they deserve.


Life-Elixir Care Foundation has grown to encompass several impactful projects, each designed to address specific societal needs. Our dedication to empowering individuals extends to the Empowering Women and the Girl Child Project. Through this initiative, we focus on providing educational opportunities, charachter building and support for girls and women, recognizing the transformative power of education in fostering social change and women's empowerment. Scholarships, vocational training, and mentorship programs form the core of our efforts, equipping women and girls with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive.


In addition to our commitment to the young and the empowerment of women, our organization places a high priority on Community Development. We work closely with local communities, actively listening to their needs and collaborating to implement projects and outreaches that foster growth and progress. Through these efforts, we aim to enhance social cohesion and resilience, ultimately contributing to the sustainable development of communities.


The Nutrition and Feeding Programs Project underscores our dedication to combating hunger and malnutrition in vulnerable communities. With the establishment of food banks and nutrition programs, we ensure that individuals and families have access to adequate and nutritious meals. Our focus on addressing food insecurity promotes better health outcomes and enhances overall well-being. By promoting healthy living and nutrition, we strive to create a healthier and more resilient community.


At Life-Elixir Care Foundation, we are driven by the vision of a brighter future for all. Our projects have been carefully crafted to meet the diverse needs of the less privileged, empowering them to lead fulfilling lives and contribute positively to society. Through education, support, and sustainable development initiatives, we aspire to uplift individuals and communities, leaving a lasting impact on the world around us. As we continue our journey, we remain committed to making the world a better place for everyone, one project at a time.
