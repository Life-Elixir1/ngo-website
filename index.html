Welcome to Life-Elixir Care Foundation, a distinguished non-governmental organization based in Nigeria. Our unwavering vision is rooted in compassionate care for the poor and indigent, accomplished through two vital avenues: empowering young individuals with essential resources to break the chains of poverty and providing fundamental support to both the young and elderly.


Our Commitment:

At Life-Elixir Care Foundation, we are committed to transforming lives and communities through holistic interventions. By fostering skill acquisition trainings, offering financial support for startups and business expansion, financing education for the less privileged, and fostering partnerships with organizations dedicated to caring for the elderly and indigent, we strive to bring about meaningful change.

Empowering Youth:

We firmly believe that empowering young people is pivotal to building a prosperous future. Through comprehensive skill acquisition trainings, we equip them with practical expertise and knowledge, empowering them to overcome adversity and create sustainable livelihoods. By investing in their potential, we enable them to become catalysts for positive change within their communities.

Caring for the Vulnerable:

Our dedication to providing basic care extends to both the young and old. By identifying and partnering with organizations that specialize in offering comprehensive support to the indigent and elderly, we ensure that their needs are met with utmost compassion and respect. Together, we strive to improve the quality of life for those most in need, fostering a society where no one is left behind.


Join Our Cause:

Life-Elixir Care Foundation welcomes individuals and organizations alike to join us on this remarkable journey of impact and transformation. Together, we can uplift lives, break down barriers, and create a society where every person thrives.
As we work tirelessly towards our vision, we remain steadfast in our belief that by caring for the poor and indigent, we can sow the seeds of a brighter future. With your support, we can truly make a difference and forge a path towards a more inclusive and compassionate society.

Contact us

If you or you know of anyone in dire need of assistance for basic education (primary, secondary and in some cases university education), please reach out to us via email at lifeelixircarefoundation@gmail.com.

We also offer financial assistance to young graduates who intend to start or expand their business ideas (terms and conditions apply). This is offered based on availability of funds. At least one business is funded per year. Please check our Ongoing Activities page to find out more about our initiatives and activities.
