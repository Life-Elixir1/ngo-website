Welcome to Life-Elixir Care Foundation

Life-Elixir Care Foundation is a distinguished non-governmental organization based in Nigeria. Our unwavering vision is rooted in compassionate care for the poor and indigent, accomplished through two vital avenues: empowering young individuals with essential resources to break the chains of poverty and providing fundamental support to both the young and elderly.

Join Our Cause

Life-Elixir Care Foundation welcomes individuals and organizations alike to join us on this remarkable journey of impact and transformation. Together, we can uplift lives, break down barriers, and create a society where every person thrives.
