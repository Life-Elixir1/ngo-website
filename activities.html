Education Support


One of our core activities is paying school fees for primary and secondary education. We believe that education is a powerful tool for empowerment and strive to ensure that underprivileged children have access to quality education.

We consider several factors when selecting candidates for educational support, including financial need, academic potential, and commitment to education. By offering scholarships to Nigerian institutions, we aim to empower deserving individuals to pursue their educational aspirations and unlock their full potential. Our team diligently performs background investigation on the eligibility of all sponsored candidates. School fees will be paid directly to the educational institution from the organization's account.

Entrepreneurship Support


We fund viable startup and business expansion ideas for young graduates. Through a rigorous selection process, we identify promising ventures and provide financial support, mentoring, and guidance to help these aspiring entrepreneurs establish and grow their businesses.

We carefully evaluate business proposals based on factors such as viability, scalability, market potential, and social impact. Our aim is to support innovative ideas that have the potential to create employment opportunities and contribute to economic growth. Specific information and criteria will be provided alongside the publication for applications.

Health Care Support


Our partners have well-qualified doctors and nurses, ensuring that the elderly are well taken care of. We also provide state-of-the-art homes to ensure the elderly receive the best care and attention they deserve.
