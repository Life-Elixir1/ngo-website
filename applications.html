Empowering Dreams: Introducing the Life-Elixir Care Foundation Sponsorship Grant

Are you a visionary entrepreneur looking to make a difference in society? At Life-Elixir Care Foundation, we believe in empowering dreams that have the potential to transform lives. We are excited to announce our non-profit NGO's exclusive Sponsorship Grant, specially designed to help aspiring businesses flourish and make a positive impact.

Our Commitment:

As a non-profit organization, our primary goal is to support and uplift businesses with a focus on social impact. We are dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship and community development through our Sponsorship Grant Program.

Why Choose Us?

  • Nurturing Partnerships: Life-Elixir Care Foundation aims to form nurturing partnerships with selected businesses, providing ongoing guidance and mentorship.
  • Financial Boost: Unlock your business's potential with funding of up to three million naira (#3,000,000) disbursed in three convenient installments.
  • Supportive Installments: Our flexible installment plan includes 50% upon selection and business registration, followed by 25% at six months, and the final 25% at nine months, contingent on reasonable progress.
  • Double Opportunity: Embrace the opportunity to receive our Sponsorship Grant twice, doubling your potential for success.

Selection Criteria:

At Life-Elixir Care Foundation, we seek businesses with a clear focus on social impact. We evaluate applications based on the following criteria:

  • Thoroughly researched business plan showcasing your passion and dedication.
  • Innovative and viable business ideas with the potential for sustainable growth.
  • Proximity to preferred locations (Aba, Port Harcourt and Owerri) to facilitate engagement.
  • The promise of job creation and a positive contribution to society.

Application Details:

The application period opens today (xx/xx/2023) and lasts for 15 days from this announcement. Submit your impactful business plan as a PDF document to admin@life-elixircarefoundation.de to seize this life-changing opportunity.
