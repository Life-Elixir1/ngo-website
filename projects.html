Education Support Project


The Education Support Project focuses on providing scholarships to underprivileged children who have the potential to excel academically. By supporting their education, we aim to empower them to become future leaders and change agents in their communities.

Entrepreneurship Support Project


The Entrepreneurship Support Project identifies and supports promising business ideas from young entrepreneurs. We provide funding, mentorship, and resources to help them establish and grow successful businesses, contributing to economic development and job creation.

Health Care Support Project


The Health Care Support Project aims to improve healthcare access for the less privileged and the elderly. We collaborate with healthcare institutions to provide quality medical care, ensuring the well-being of those in need.
