Education Support Project


The Education Support Project focuses on providing scholarships to underprivileged children who have the potential to excel academically. By supporting their education, we aim to empower them to become future leaders and change agents in their communities.

Entrepreneurship Support Project


The Entrepreneurship Support Project identifies and supports promising business ideas from young entrepreneurs. We provide funding, mentorship, and resources to help them establish and grow successful businesses, contributing to economic development and job creation.

Health Care Support Project


The Health Care Support Project aims to improve healthcare access for the less privileged and the elderly. We collaborate with healthcare institutions to provide quality medical care, ensuring the well-being of those in need.

Our Commitment:

As a non-profit organization, our primary goal is to support and uplift businesses with a focus on social impact. We are dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship and community development through our Sponsorship Grant Program.

Why Choose Us?

  • Nurturing Partnerships: Life-Elixir Care Foundation aims to form nurturing partnerships with selected businesses, providing ongoing guidance and mentorship.
  • Financial Boost: Unlock your business's potential with funding of up to three million naira (#3,000,000) disbursed in three convenient installments.

At Life-Elixir Care Foundation, we seek businesses with a clear focus on social impact. We evaluate applications based on the following criteria:

  • Proximity to preferred locations (Aba, Port Harcourt and Owerri) to facilitate engagement.
  • The promise of job creation and a positive contribution to society.

Application Requirements for Sponsorship Grant

Executive Summary:

Provide a concise overview of your business, including its mission, vision, and objectives. Highlight the key features of your products/services and the target market.

Detailed Business Plan:

Present a comprehensive business plan that covers the following aspects:

  • a. Business Description: Describe the nature of your business, its industry, and the problem it seeks to solve.
  • b. Products/Services: Provide a clear and detailed description of the products or services you offer, emphasizing their unique selling points and competitive advantage.
  • c. Sales Strategy: Outline your sales and marketing strategy, including target audience identification, distribution channels, and promotional activities.
  • d. Financial Projections: Include a six-month profitability analysis, showcasing projected revenue, expenses, and expected profits. Explain the assumptions and methodologies used in your financial forecasts.

Purpose of Grant Application:

Clearly state the reason for seeking the sponsorship grant. Explain how the grant will specifically contribute to the growth and development of your business. Highlight any significant challenges that the grant will help address.

Business Impact and Benefits:

Demonstrate the positive impact your business will have on the community or industry. Emphasize any potential job creation, economic growth, and social benefits that may arise from the successful implementation of your business plan.

Employment Projection:

Estimate the number of employees you expect to hire and the roles they will play in your business. Provide insights into your hiring plan, including how these employees will contribute to the business's growth and success.

Budget and Funding Allocation:

Present a detailed budget outlining the allocation of the sponsorship grant funds. Specify how the grant money will be used to support various aspects of your business, such as product development, marketing, equipment acquisition, or workforce expansion.

Business Viability and Sustainability:

Demonstrate the long-term viability and sustainability of your business model. Explain how your business plans to adapt to market changes and maintain profitability beyond the initial grant period.

Previous Achievements and Milestones:

If possible, highlight any significant achievements, awards, or milestones your business has reached. Provide evidence of your business's track record, indicating its potential for success in the future.

Collaborations and Partnerships:

If applicable, showcase any existing partnerships or collaborations that enhance the credibility and potential of your business.

Compliance and Legal Requirements:

Ensure that your business complies with all relevant laws, regulations, and licensing requirements. Include documentation (e.g. CAC registration) to validate your compliance and demonstrate your commitment to operating responsibly.
