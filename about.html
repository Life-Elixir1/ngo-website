About Us

Life-Elixir Care Foundation aims to support and provide financial assistance to the indigent, thereby alleviating the struggles of less privileged individuals. Our organization is dedicated to finding and making a positive impact on the lives of those in need through various initiatives. Visit our activities page to find out more about our previous and ongoing initiatives.

Our Objectives

  • Provide financial assistance and support to the less privileged, offering relief from their daily hardships.
  • Grant scholarships to underprivileged individuals, ensuring access to quality education.
  • Promote and support the healthcare needs of the less privileged.
  • Empower youth, less privileged individuals, and the indigent with skill acquisition and capacity-building programs, enabling them to break free from poverty's grip.
  • Provide adequate care for the elderly.

Collaboration and Partnership

We actively collaborate with like-minded individuals, government agencies, local organizations, and international partners to achieve our objectives and make a substantial difference in the lives of the less privileged.
