Welcome to Life-Elixir Care Foundation!


At Life-Elixir Care Foundation, we believe in the power of undying hope and unconditional love. Just like the elixir of life, representing the immortality of Christ and His boundless love for humanity, we strive to extend His arms of love to those in need, bringing hope to seemingly hopeless situations. Our mission is to make life worth living for everyone and to create a better world by making a positive impact on the lives of the less privileged.

Our diverse programs are strategically planned to address various societal challenges and contribute to the attainment of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Visit our activities page to find out more about our previous and ongoing initiatives.

Our Objectives


  • Charity and Financial Assistance
  • Nutrition and Feeding Programs
  • Geriatric Care
  • Women and Girl Child Empowerment
  • Leadership Training and Skill Acquisition
  • ICT Training and Mentorship
  • Community Development

Collaboration and Partnership


We actively collaborate with like-minded individuals, government agencies, local organizations, and international partners to achieve our objectives and make a substantial difference in the lives of the less privileged.
